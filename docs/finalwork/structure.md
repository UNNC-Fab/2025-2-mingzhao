## 3. Structure Diagram

This section details the physical construction of the sand table, including mechanical components and assembly.
![](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jasperxi0218/imageuploadservice/main/img/20260131143515973.png)
### 3.1 Mechanical Architecture

A high-level view of the table's structural layers:

- **Top Layer**: The sand bed and miniature interaction area.
- **Middle Layer**: Sensor arrays (OAK Camera mounting) and actuation mechanisms.
- **Base Layer**: Housing for Arduino/ESP32 controllers and power supply.

### 3.2 Assembly & Exploded View

The 3D CAD models used for fabrication.

- **Laser-cut Parts**: Box structure and internal supports.
- **3D-printed Parts**: Custom brackets for the OAK camera and servo mounts.
